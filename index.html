About

I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at Harvard University, affiliated with the John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS). I am co-advised by Prof. Na Li and Prof. Sham Kakade.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the theoretical foundations of intelligent systems and decision making, with a focus on multi-agent systems, game theory, online control, reinforcement learning, and machine learning.
